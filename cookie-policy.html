Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you access and use our Website. They allow our Website to recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions. Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use both session cookies and persistent cookies on our Website:

  • Persistent cookies remain on your device until they expire or you delete them. They help us recognize you as a returning user to our Website.
